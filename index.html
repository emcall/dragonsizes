FAQ

Why won't the form let me add commas for the decimals?

In English decimal places are indicated by periods, not commas. So 1.1 instead of 1,1.

Sometimes a dragon sticks to my cursor, is this going to be fixed?

This glitch is the bane of my existence. Sometimes it doesn't catch when you release the mouse button and I don't know why. If you can figure it out please tell me the solution, I will give you dragon money as a reward.

The wings on one of my dragons are misplaced. They're on the butt/neck. Why?

Congrats on having a dragon with silly proportions! When FR creates a new dragon it appears to determine length and wingspan separately, so you can have a dragon that's unusally large with unusually small wings, or vice versa. Wing placement is based roughly off of average proportions, so if the wings are proportionally very small they get pushed up to the neck, and very large ones get pushed to the butt. Not much to be done about it.

Is there a way to change the color of a dragon?

The way it's set up now, no. I have an idea to make this possible later but it's backburner.

How do I zoom in/out?

Hold Ctrl/Cmd and hit = to zoom in, - to zoom out.

A new dragon released, when can I expect it to be added?
